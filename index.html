About Me

Career Summary

I am a results-driven MIS and RTR Analyst with 3.6 years of hands-on experience in financial reporting, data analysis, and management information systems. I specialize in building insightful Power BI dashboards and automating Excel-based reports to support executive decision-making.

My expertise spans the full Record-to-Report cycle — from journal entries and trial balance reviews to month-end closings and P&L commentary. I have a strong command of SAP, Power Query, SQL, and VBA, enabling me to deliver accurate, timely reports across finance teams.

I am committed to continuous improvement and have consistently contributed to DSO reduction, reporting automation, and cross-functional analytics initiatives.
